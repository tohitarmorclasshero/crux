# Heritage-Dragonborn

#### Dragonborn
Requires: Str1+, Cha1+
- **Cursed**: You were human once. You're immune to certain curses that affect your physical form.
- **Dragon-blooded**: You drank the blood of the dragon, and were reborn in the hatchery.
